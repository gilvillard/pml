timing init FFT tables + DIF evaluate
columns:
order	init	init_pr	eval	eval_v2	eval_v3	eval_v4

-> order is fft level; length of fft is 2**order
-> init is computing tables of powers of w
-> init_pr is the same powers + for each of them, precomputed values for multiplication by this
-> eval, eval_v2, eval_v3, eval_v4 are the timings for different variants of radix2 DIF:
    --> excluding the time for precomputing powers of w;
    --> rather naive implementations (no delayed reductions);
    --> only v4 is exploiting the precomputations on the powers of w for faster multiplication
